This session will cover details about all the resources prvided by the web service.

GET
  • team - Resource fetches all the details about the teams present. and the details of the links for getting and maintaining the data of players.
  • team/{teamName}/player - Resource fetches all the details of the player of a particular team.
  • team/{teamName}/player/{playerId} - Resource fetches the details of a particular player with player id mentioned.
POST
  • team/{teamName}/player- Resource creates a new player entry in the players table.
PATCH
  • team/{teamName}/player/{playerId}- Resource updates the details of a player entry in the players table.
DELETE
  • team/{teamName}/player/{playerId}- Resource deletes the details of a player entry in the players table.
the avaliable teams are:- india,UK and US
